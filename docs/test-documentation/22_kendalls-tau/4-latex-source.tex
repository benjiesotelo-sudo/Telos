$\tau$ (Kendall’s tau-b) is a rank correlation based on concordant vs. discordant pairs, with a correction for ties --- well suited to small samples and many ties. Sign = direction, magnitude = strength; p tests significance. Your significance threshold ($\alpha$) is 0.05.

A Kendall's tau-b correlation gave $\tau$=.75 [.66, .82], p $<$ .001, N=40.
